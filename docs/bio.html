Biography

Harvey Dale - Lead Singer

Harvey Dale grew up in Austin, Texas. Known as the lead singer with a booming voice and a wild stage presence, he's the band's frontman.

Lola Steppe - Bass

Known as the eccentric bassist from NYC, Lola masters the rhythm of the band.

Ricky Stone - Drummer

The powerhouse drummer that is the heartbeat of the band. Also a fellow Austinite, Ricky learned to play the drums from a young age, and has been banging rhythms out since he could crawl.
